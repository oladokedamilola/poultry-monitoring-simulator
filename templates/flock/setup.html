How the Simulation System Works
Realistic Data Generation

Our simulator creates realistic temperature, humidity, ammonia, and activity data based on your flock's characteristics and time of day.

24/7 Monitoring

Once started, the simulation runs continuously, mimicking real-world conditions with day/night cycles and environmental changes.

Smart Alert System

Receive instant notifications when conditions go outside safe ranges for your specific breed and age group.

Data Storage & Analysis

All simulated data is stored for analysis, helping you understand patterns and optimize flock management.

Breed-Specific Monitoring

Different poultry breeds have different optimal conditions. Our system adjusts monitoring parameters based on your selected breed:

Broilers Optimal temperature: 29-32°C
Layers Optimal temperature: 20-24°C
Kuroilers Hardy, adaptable birds
Local Breeds Traditional farm birds
Age-Specific Care
Chicks (0-8 weeks)

Require higher temperatures and careful monitoring during critical growth phase.

Growers (8-20 weeks)

Rapid growth phase requiring optimal feed conversion monitoring.

Adults (20+ weeks)

Mature birds focused on production (eggs or weight gain).
